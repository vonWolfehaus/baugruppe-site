What's Baugruppen?

Baugruppen is German for "Building Groups" which is essentially urban cohousing, where like-minded individuals and/or families come together to design, build, and inhabit a condominium in a city environment.
Learn about the process

Do you wish you could...

...live comfortably without a car?
You can live in a dense urban area where groceries, parks, schools, and restaurants are all within walking distance.
...enjoy a robust sense of community?
The neighborhood can be structured in such a way as to encourage casual encounters with neighbors. There could be a large common area with a big kitchen, eating space, and lounge, where families could take turns making meals for the whole group.
...live sustainably?
Most baugruppen are built to passivhaus standards, with tons of insulation, natural light, and fresh air circulation. They can have solar panels, batteries, and smart appliances, all networked together by a smart whole-home energy management system. It would create more energy than it consumes. It would have the ability to island itself from the grid in the case of emergency.
